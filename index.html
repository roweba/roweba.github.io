Welcome to my page! This website is hosted on GitHub Pages.

My name is Rowena Zhu and I am a third year Computer Science student at the University of Victoria. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Science - Software Engineering option with Co-op (a mouthful). I have previous experience working with the BC provincial government, focusing on networks. This was an amazing opportunity, as it really expanded my horizons. I was exposed to office culture, and learned a great deal about computer networking. For my next co-op I am looking to focus on development.

I believe programming is such a fundamental skill to have, which is not emphasized enough in the world today. This has prompted me to share my knowlege and love of technology with others. I currently work as a Youth Stem Instructor at Science Venture, and teach children code. I have also volunteered with Ladies Learning Code, and UVic computer science clubs.

I am interested software development and computer networking. In my classes, I have learned various elements of programming skills, algorithms/data structures, software engineering principles, computer networking, and computer hardware. I have always loved creating new things, and the way programming allows me to do that is one of the main reasons I chose my major.

Aside from programming, I am interested in music, art, history, literature, and world cultures.
