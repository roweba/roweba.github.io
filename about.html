Why Computer Science?

The first exposure I had to coding was in Grade 10 when I was 15. I had just dropped a class and needed a new elective, but the only available one was an upper level programming class. I decided to try it out, as I had never coded before and figured it would be interesting to try out (I also had no choice). I ended up struggling with the class and got the lowest grade I have ever received in a high school class. After that, I was put off coding for a while, until I was required to take another computer science course in first year University. I took a lot of general courses in my first year, and realized that out of all of them I actually enjoyed my coding classes the most. I decided to change my major, and never looked back!
